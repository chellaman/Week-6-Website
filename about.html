I am an eighteen-year-old, deaf, queer, genderqueer, Chinese/Jewish artist.

My inspiration is anything I stumble upon that I find aesthetically pleasing.

This could be a Picasso in the MoMA or a polka-dotted hat on the NY subway.

Mainly, however, I create my art by extracting my emotions from life experiences.
